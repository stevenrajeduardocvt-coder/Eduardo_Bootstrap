Death Note

Death Note is my favorite anime of all time.

Death Note is a psychological masterpiece that explores intelligence, morality, and power. It follows Light Yagami, a brilliant student who discovers a notebook capable of killing anyone whose name is written inside it.

The intense rivalry between Light and L turns every episode into a strategic battle, questioning justice and the dangers of absolute power. Its dark atmosphere, iconic characters, and unforgettable soundtrack make it timeless.
